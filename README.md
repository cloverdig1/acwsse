# Adama Water Supply & Sewerage Service Enterprise (ATWSSE)

Official website for the Adama Water Supply & Sewerage Service Enterprise, documenting the Adama Town Water Supply and Sanitation Improvement Project (ATWSSIP).

## Project Overview

The ATWSSIP is a major infrastructure project that has transformed Adama's water supply system through a dual-source network combining:
- **Awash River surface water** from Koka Dam treatment plant
- **Mojo Well Field groundwater** from 13 deep production wells

### Key Statistics
- **Total daily supply capacity**: 122,839 m³
- **Active connections (2026)**: 103,577
- **Municipal service coverage**: 94%
- **Transmission & distribution network**: 244 km
- **Total investment**: $69.7M USD + €1.30M EUR + 1.48B ETB
- **Implementation period**: 2020-2026
